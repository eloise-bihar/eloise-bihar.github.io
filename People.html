Principal Investigator

Dr. Eloise Bihar Assistant Professor, MDI

Eloise Bihar is an assistant professor in the Department of Materials Design and Innovation at the University at Buffalo. She earned her Ph.D. from École des Mines de Saint-Étienne and completed postdoctoral fellowships at KAUST (Saudi Arabia) and the University of Colorado-Boulder.

Her research focuses on hydrogel-based biosensors, soft bioelectronics, OECTs, and multifunctional cryogels for wound healing, infection monitoring, and neural interfaces.

Graduate Students

Rebekah Finster Ph.D. Student

Rebekah works on hydrogel engineering, OECT-based biosensing, and soft bioelectronics. Her research explores ion transport in cryogels and the design of hydrogel-integrated therapeutic sensing platforms.
