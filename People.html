Principal Investigator

Dr. Eloise Bihar Assistant Professor, MDI

Eloise Bihar is an assistant professor in the Department of Materials Design and Innovation at the State University of New York at Buffalo. She earned her Ph.D. from École des Mines de Saint-Étienne in France and completed two postdoctoral fellowships – one at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) in Saudi Arabia and another at the University of Colorado-Boulder.

With a background in printed electronics and sensors, her research focuses on developing innovative, low-cost biosensors and biodegradable electronic materials for applications in healthcare, environmental monitoring, and agriculture. Her work advances sustainable materials and smart sensing technologies.
