Bihar Lab

Soft Bioelectronics & Hydrogel-Based Sensors

Welcome to the Bihar Lab at the University at Buffalo. We work on bridging materials science and bioelectronics through printed adaptive hydrogels.

Research Areas

  • Hydrogel-based biosensors
  • Organic electrochemical transistors (OECTs)
  • Wound healing technologies
  • Cryogel electrolytes & ion transport
  • Bioactive materials & neural implants
  • Sensors for agriculture
